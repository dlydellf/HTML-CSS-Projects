Our Classes





Beginner

Introduction to the basics of proper stance, your bridge, the stroke, correct speed usage, aiming, follow-through, the break, and the importance of shot selection & post-shot positioning.

Intermediate

Instruction in the nuances of defensive play, banking, carmon shots, combination shots, cheating the pocket, kicking, spin, and English.

Advanced

Comprehensive guidance to help hone your understanding & execution of ALL topics covered in the Beginner & Intermediate classes, plus unlimited match play with the instructor of your choice.
